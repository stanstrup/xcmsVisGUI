### Plot views

- [TIC /
  BPC](https://stanstrup.github.io/xcmsVisGUI/articles/tic_bpc.md):

  Overlay the total- or base-peak chromatogram of every included file.

- [EIC](https://stanstrup.github.io/xcmsVisGUI/articles/eic.md):

  Extracted-ion chromatograms for one or many m/z targets across files.

- [Spectrum](https://stanstrup.github.io/xcmsVisGUI/articles/spectrum.md):

  View a single spectrum, browse the scan list, and annotate adducts,
  isotopes and in-source fragments.

- [MS map](https://stanstrup.github.io/xcmsVisGUI/articles/ms_map.md):

  A 2D m/z x retention-time map, plus 3D surface and point views.

- [Precursors](https://stanstrup.github.io/xcmsVisGUI/articles/precursors.md):

  Map every fragmented precursor ion (rt x precursor m/z) for DDA data.
